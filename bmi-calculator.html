Calculate your BMI






A body mass index higher than 30 indicates obesity.
People with this degree of obesity are at risk for
serious health conditions like heart disease and diabetes.

To calculate your BMI, use these numbers as guidelines:

  • 24 or under = ideal body weight
  • 25 to 29.9 = overweight
  • 30 or above = obese

BMI = Body Mass Index
